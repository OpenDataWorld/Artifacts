# AnyDB Theory: The Forward-Expanding Shared and Distributed Knowledge Economy

Status: Foundational Theory

## Thesis

> Reality is not a loop. It is a forward-moving, ever-expanding, shared, and distributed knowledge economy.

Every AnythingKube, AnyAgent, domain, transaction, artifact, and decision advances one governed step at a time. History is preserved. Knowledge expands. Multiple simulations may explore possibility, but only one real meta-model records what became true in the real world.

## Core Rule

> Real movement is forward-only. Simulations may branch freely. Only admitted, executed, evidenced, and reconciled outcomes enter the real meta-model of truth.

An agent does not live forever by default.

Its authority, credentials, memory access, tools, contracts, and operational existence are time-bound.

## Natural Death

Natural death is the planned completion of an agent's operational life.

It may occur because:

- its objective is fulfilled
- its contract expires
- its domain no longer requires it
- its model or runtime is obsolete
- its owner retires it
- its successor is admitted
- its lawful authority ends
- its evidence or trust expires

Natural death is not deletion of history.

The agent's operational authority ends, while its evidence, provenance, decisions, and lifecycle record remain governed according to retention and privacy rules.

## Graceful Exit

A graceful exit must:

1. stop accepting new work
2. complete, transfer, compensate, or close active work
3. revoke or expire credentials
4. close delegations
5. settle wallet obligations
6. transfer permitted memory and artifacts
7. preserve decision and evidence history
8. notify affected domains and peers
9. update trust and lifecycle state
10. publish a terminal exit event
