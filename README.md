# OpenDataWorld Artifacts

A research and publication platform for technical artifacts, specifications, protocols, architecture papers, governance frameworks, and cloud-native knowledge.

## Vision

Artifacts transforms ideas into durable knowledge.

Every article, protocol, framework, specification, and research paper becomes a versioned artifact that can be reviewed, cited, governed, and evolved.
